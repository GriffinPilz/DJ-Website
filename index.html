About UpTone

Young as he may be, UpTone approaches mainstream EDM with the maturity of a more traditional DJ. He picks and chooses the best of what numerous styles offer, seamlessly blending them into soundscapes sure to set a crowd into motion.

Edits, mashups and reworks largely characterize each of his high-energy performances, and rock and hip-hop songs find their way into his track list as a result. Above all else, he takes it upon himself to find the common ground between every pair of feet on the dance floor.
